Pancakes

Description

This batter makes light, fluffy, and delicious pancakes. I have been making them for years and everyone that tries them wants the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/2 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Steps

  1. Sift the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt into a large bowl.
  2. Whisk the water and oil together in a small bowl.
  3. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients, and pour in the wet. Stir just until blended; mixture will be lumpy.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle over medium-high heat.
  5. Drop batter by large spoonfuls onto the griddle, and cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry.
  6. Flip, and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.
