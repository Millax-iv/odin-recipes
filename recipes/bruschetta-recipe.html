Bruschetta

Description

Simple appetizer - the balsamic vinegar gives it a little kick. Best if the mixture has 1 or 2 hours to blend flavours before serving.

Ingredients

  • 1 roma(plum) tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 tablespoon and 1/2 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2 tablespoon shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3/8 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1/8 teaspoon olive oil
  • kosher salt
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • .13 loaf french, toasted and sliced

Steps

  1. In a bowl toss together the tomatoes, basil, cheese and garlic.
  2. Mix in the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, kosher salt and pepper.
  3. Serve on toasted bread.
